Dental health is an often-overlooked part of pet care. Regular teeth brushing between vet cleanings helps prevent plaque buildup, gum disease, and that dreaded doggy breath.

Our groomers use pet-safe enzymatic toothpaste in flavors your pet will enjoy — making the experience as pleasant as possible.
