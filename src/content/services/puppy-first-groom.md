Starting grooming early sets your puppy up for a lifetime of stress-free grooming experiences. Our puppy package focuses on building trust and positive associations with the grooming process.

We take things slow, use lots of treats and praise, and never push your puppy past their comfort level. This introductory session is for puppies under 6 months old.
