Jessica founded Paws & Polish with a simple mission: to provide the kind of grooming experience she always wanted for her own pets. With over 12 years of professional grooming experience and certifications from the National Dog Groomers Association of America, she brings expertise and genuine love for animals to every appointment.

When she's not grooming, Jessica is volunteering at the local animal shelter and competing in grooming competitions.
