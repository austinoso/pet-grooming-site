question: "What is your cancellation policy?"
category: "booking"
order: 6
---

We understand that plans change. We ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Cancellations with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee. No-shows may be charged the full service price. Please see our Policies page for full details.
