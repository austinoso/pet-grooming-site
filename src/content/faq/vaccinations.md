---
question: "Does my pet need to be up to date on vaccinations?"
category: "health"
order: 4
---

Yes — for the safety of all pets in our care, we require that dogs be current on rabies, distemper, and bordetella (kennel cough) vaccinations. Cats must be current on rabies and FVRCP. Please bring proof of vaccination to your first appointment, or have your vet's office send records to us in advance.
