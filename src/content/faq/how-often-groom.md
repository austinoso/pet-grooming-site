---
question: "How often should I have my dog groomed?"
category: "general"
order: 1
---

Most dogs benefit from professional grooming every 4–6 weeks, though this varies by breed, coat type, and lifestyle. Dogs with longer coats or those who spend a lot of time outdoors may need more frequent grooming, while short-haired breeds can often go longer between appointments. We're happy to recommend a personalized schedule during your first visit.
